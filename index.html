About KCNails UKR

KCNails UKR was founded with a simple mission: to democratize professional-quality nail products and make them accessible to everyone. What started as a passion project in a small studio has grown into a trusted brand serving thousands of nail enthusiasts across the globe. Our founder, a professional nail artist, recognized a gap in the market for high-quality, affordable products that didn't compromise on excellence. Today, we remain committed to that original vision, continuously innovating and expanding our product line to meet the evolving needs of our community.

At the heart of KCNails UKR lies our core belief: nail art is a form of self-expression and creativity that should be available to everyone. We're dedicated to empowering our customers with the tools, products, and inspiration they need to create stunning nail art. Our commitment extends beyond products to building a supportive community where artists of all skill levels can share, learn, and grow together. We stand by our values of quality, ethics, and customer satisfaction in everything we do.

Applying our nail decals is simple and straightforward! First, prepare your nails with a base coat and let it dry completely. Then, select the decal size that matches your nail, carefully peel it from the backing, and apply it to your nail. Smooth out any air bubbles gently with your fingernail or a soft brush. Finally, seal everything with a top coat for protection and longevity. Our decals are designed to adhere smoothly and last as long as your polish.
